Overview

In the recent events in the world, it has shown us how important community is to each and everyone’s mental wellbeing. For many wine has been the component to bring communities and friends together. During these unprecedented times people became even more creative, from having virtual happy hours, backyard wine nights and even wine walks around the neighborhood.


Our inspiration consited of:
- Bringing the taste of wine country into your own home. Can we have the quality and taste without the cost of traveling?
- In these times of quarantine and staycations, we are looking for our data to bring an informed decision when looking for a wine based on type and rating of points.
- Wine Enthusiast Magazine often showcases many different varieties of wine that we have never tried.


Utilizing the Wine Reviews data set from kaggle.com we were able to analyze over 120K wine reviews. The data consisted of the type of wine, rating, location and winery for different varieties of wine. Utilizing our resources, we were able to leverage a previous Boot Camp project to obtain categorization of each wine type into a Red Wine or White Wine category. We were also able to utilize the previous project’s work to obtain latitude and longitudes for each province.
