Wine Provinces

Have you ever sat around and asked yourself I wonder where your wine is made or if certain regions were more popular than others?


Part of our wine analysis led us to determining the most popular regions around the world that produces wine. We analyzed over 120K data entries and took the top 100 Red Wines and 100 White Wines to determine if there was a correlation between ratings and geographical locations. The Top Wine Provinces Chart is an interactive chart that will allow you to navigate through the types of wine all the way down the most popular regions the wines are produced. Just click on the any of the two outer rings to retrieve additional information, to navigate back up any layers just click in the center of the pie chart.
